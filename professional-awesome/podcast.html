About the Show

What We Talk About

The Professional Awesome Podcast covers everything from the nitty-gritty of race car engineering to the stories behind the builds. We talk to drivers, engineers, shop owners, and enthusiasts who share our passion for going fast.

🏎️ Race Recaps

Post-event breakdowns of what went right, what went wrong, and what we learned at each race weekend.

🔧 Tech Deep Dives

Engineering topics explained — from aerodynamics theory to suspension kinematics to data analysis.

🎤 Guest Interviews

Conversations with the people who make motorsport happen — drivers, engineers, organizers, and more.

📢 Industry News

Hot takes on what's happening in the world of grassroots motorsport and performance parts.
