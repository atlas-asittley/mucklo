# Welcome to Professional Awesome Racing

We're thrilled to launch our new website! After years on WordPress, we've moved to a faster, cleaner platform that puts our content and products front and center.

## What's New

The new site features:

- **Faster loading** — No more bloated WordPress plugins. Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- **Better shopping experience** — Browse parts by category, add to cart, and check out seamlessly.
- **Tech Info hub** — We've organized all our technical knowledge into easy-to-browse sections.
- **Mobile-first design** — The site looks great on your phone, because that's where most of you browse.
