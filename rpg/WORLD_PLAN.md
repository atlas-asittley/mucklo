# World Map Expansion & Item System — Master Plan

## Phase 1: World Map Expansion ✅
Add 5 new areas to the world map. Each area has:
- Unique visual theme
- Enemy types appropriate to the area
- 1-2 enemy tiers that scale with player level
- At least one shop or NPC

**Areas:** Volcanic Wastes, Frozen Peaks, Shadow Forest, Dwarf Fortress, Ruins of Aethoria ✅

## Phase 2: Damage Type System ✅
Introduce 6 damage types: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Poison, Holy, Dark
- Enemy weaknesses and resistances
- Damage type display in combat log
- Player resist gear

## Phase 3: Proc/Effect Items ✅
Items that trigger effects on attack or hit.

**Proc Types:**
- `onAttack` — chance to cast effects (fireball, freeze, etc.)
- `onHit` — chance to heal, shield, or apply effects
- `whileEquipped` — passive damage added to attacks

## Phase 4: Type Stacking & Synergies ✅
Encourage combining matching damage type items.

**Synergy Bonuses (when multiple items of same type equipped):**
- 2 items: +5% matching damage bonus
- 3+ items: +10% matching damage + aura effect

**6 Element Sets:** Fire, Ice, Lightning, Poison, Holy, Dark
