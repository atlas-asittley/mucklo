Dentistry With Heart,

Mill Creek Family Dental has served the Lafayette community since 2004. Dr. John Matthew Cronkhite, DDS believes great dental care starts with a relationship — we take the time to listen, explain your options, and always discuss diagnosis and costs before proceeding. No surprises.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine cleanings to complete smile makeovers, we offer a full range of dental services to keep your family's smiles healthy and beautiful.

Cleanings & Oral Exams

Professional cleanings, comprehensive exams, oral cancer screenings, and sealants to keep your smile healthy and catch problems early.

Crowns & Bridgework

Custom-crafted dental crowns and bridges to restore damaged teeth and replace missing ones with natural-looking, long-lasting results.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements that look and function like your own teeth — the gold standard in tooth replacement.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, cosmetic crowns, and tooth-colored fillings to give you the confident, beautiful smile you deserve.

Root Canal Treatment

Modern root canal therapy is comfortable and effective. We save your natural tooth, eliminate pain, and restore your smile quickly.

Extractions

Gentle tooth extractions performed with care and precision, including support for tooth decay prevention and cavity-free outcomes.

Removable Dentures

Full and partial removable dentures custom-fitted for comfort, function, and a natural appearance to restore your complete smile.

TMD Treatments

Relief for jaw pain, clicking, and bite problems. We assess TMJ disorders and provide targeted treatments to restore comfort and function.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies don't wait. Contact us for urgent toothaches, broken teeth, and other emergency needs — we're here to help.

Not sure what you need? Call us and we'll help you find the right treatment.
